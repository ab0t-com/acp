# ACP — Branding & Marketing Image Prompts (v2 · cute cartoon)

**Date:** 2026-06-10 · Alternate art direction to v1. Same 15 use-cases + shapes (drop-in),
but reimagined as **cute flat-vector cartoon — Japanese kawaii meets clean American
corporate product-launch.** Each prompt is an image-gen brief written *to a human artist*,
layer-by-layer, with the ideal shape for the reshape-checker.

---

## Brand bible v2 (read first — every image obeys this)

**Product (unchanged).** ACP — *Agent Coordination Protocol*: a shared filesystem + comms
line so multiple AI agents on different machines collaborate. By **ab0t** (ab0t.com).

**The v2 vibe.** Adorable but *premium*. Picture a polished product-launch keyframe from a
Japanese tech brand (LINE Friends, Rakuten mascots, Studio-soft) crossed with the clean
flat confidence of an American SaaS launch (Slack/Notion/Duolingo/Mailchimp). **Cute,
simple, friendly, trustworthy, joyful — "you'll love using this."** Approachable, never
childish-cheap; still a serious tool, just delightful.

**The mascots (the heart of v2).** Two tiny friendly **agent buddies** — soft rounded
capsule/bean characters with simple dot eyes, a tiny happy mouth, and little nub hands:
- **Teal Buddy** — mint-teal body, calm and helpful.
- **Ember Buddy** — warm coral-orange body, eager and friendly.
They are best friends who *work together*. When they sync, a small **violet sync-star** ✦
pops between them (the coordination/handshake spark). They share a little glowing **canvas/
screen** (the shared filesystem). Keep them SIMPLE: 2 dot eyes, minimal mouth, no noses, no
fingers — think emoji-simple, mascot-clean. Consistent proportions across all images.

**Style rules.** Flat vector illustration. Big rounded shapes, thick soft outlines (or
clean outline-less), ONE soft drop shadow max, minimal flat shading (no heavy gradients).
Limited palette, lots of cheerful negative space, gentle confetti/sparkle accents used
sparingly. Light/airy by default (cream or soft-mint backgrounds) — bright product-release
energy, not the dark v1 mood.

**Palette (simple, flat — 5–6 colors max per image).**
- Backgrounds: warm cream `#FFF6EA` / soft mint `#EAF7F3` / pale sky `#EAF1FB`.
- Teal Buddy: `#3FD0B8`. Ember Buddy: `#FF9466`. Sync-star + accents: violet `#8B7BF0`.
- Ink (outlines/eyes/text): soft charcoal `#2B2F36`. Optional pop: butter yellow `#FFD66B`.

**Type (if shown).** Friendly rounded geometric sans (Quicksand / Nunito / rounded-Inter
feel), lowercase, cheerful. Keep text minimal — usually leave clean space and we set it.

**Always avoid.** Scary/complex robots, realistic rendering, creepy faces, dark moody
scenes, heavy gradients, gritty textures, clutter, too many characters, anything off-brand-
sad. If it isn't instantly cute AND clean, it's wrong.

**Judging mindset.** Like a character-design + product-keyframe competition: one clear
adorable idea, charming composition, warm emotion, instantly on-brand, value obvious.

## 0. Mascot character sheet — the on-model reference (render this FIRST)
**SHAPE:** 3:2 horizontal, 1800×1200, clean even layout, generous margins; the whole sheet
on one flat backdrop.

The single most important asset: a **character reference sheet** that locks the two buddies
so every other image stays on-model. Render this before anything else, then reuse the exact
shapes/colors everywhere. **Layers / contents:** (1) a flat off-white/cream backdrop with a
thin baseline; (2) **Teal Buddy** and **Ember Buddy** standing side by side, full-body, at
matching scale — soft rounded bean/capsule bodies, two simple dot eyes, a tiny happy mouth,
little nub hands, no nose/fingers; clearly label each with a small name + hex chip
(`Teal #3FD0B8`, `Ember #FF9466`); (3) a small **turnaround** row for each buddy: front,
3/4, side, back, kept dead simple; (4) an **expression set** row: happy, big smile + wave,
thinking (small ✦ thought dot), thumbs-up, sleeping (zzz, for failover), and "syncing"
(eyes as little stars) ; (5) the shared props drawn once: the glowing rounded **canvas-
screen** and the **violet sync-star ✦** (`#8B7BF0`); (6) a tidy palette strip at the bottom
with all 5–6 brand swatches + the charcoal ink. Flat vector, thick soft rounded outlines,
one soft shadow, perfectly consistent proportions. Style note for the artist: think a
premium mascot brand sheet (LINE Friends / Duolingo) — adorable, simple, instantly lovable.
This sheet is the source of truth for buddies #1–#15.

## Production notes (for the reshape-checker + pipeline)
- **Target shapes are explicit per prompt** (aspect + px); validate aspect first, smart-crop
  toward the focal area (most have edge negative space), never stretch.
- **Consistency anchors across all 15:** the two buddies (teal + ember) + the violet
  sync-star ✦ + the shared glowing canvas; flat vector, thick soft rounded shapes, one soft
  shadow, limited flat palette, light/airy backgrounds, lots of cheerful negative space, no
  baked-in text unless noted.
- **Keep the mascots identical** across images (same proportions, eyes, colors) — this IS the
  brand. Generate a quick mascot turnaround/reference first, then reuse it everywhere.
- **Deliver each in:** the native shape + a 2:1 (1280×640) and 1:1 crop where composition
  allows, so one render serves repo + social + icon.
- **File homes:** winners → `PUBLIC_REPO/assets/`; source prompts (this file) stay private.
